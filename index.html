About the Author

Gerald Zgabay

"No matter what you believe, what you have done or are doing - no one is discounted from God's Love and everyone is on a journey."

Gerald brings a welcoming transparency and authenticity to some of the most difficult of struggles in life. His approach is raw, informative and encouraging. Gerald is not a pastor, well he is an online ordained minster, but he has conducted weddings, funerals, spoken at churches, trained leadership, and lead house churches. And don't be surprised to hear some conversations about the paranormal, conspiracy, or the supernatural. Gerald believes anyone can go from a life of hangovers to Jesus.

Tabby Zgabay

“How wonderful is it that we laugh, because our bodies cannot contain the joy."

Tabby is an amazing, dynamic speaker who specializes in bring joy to the chaos. She is talented showing people that there is “funny” to be found in the struggles and challenges of life. While Tabby is funny, uplifting, and positive – she does this all by respecting, supporting, equipping, and encouraging those during their struggles and challenges.

The Zgabay Family

Hangover to Jesus is not just a book, it is a journey. Gerald and Tabby bring people into that journey by sharing the intimate details of their first-hand experience of restoring their own lives and marriage. They seamlessly work with one another speaking and engaging with each other while bringing the audience along for the journey. Gerald and Tabby tackle the often taboo and secretive subject matters of pornography addiction, violation of marital vows, and a hurting wives heart. They have the unique ability to bring awareness and apply hope into the most desolate of marriages.

They speak on other topics that are equal important and often uncomfortable to have conversations about. They speak on social and race injustices, transracial adoption, faith challenges, and experiencing the Holy Spirit.

Gerald and Tabby unapologetically share their experiences with the pain, lies, broken trust, and the redeeming power of the Holy Spirit within their marriage that was restored. They create an atmosphere of transparency and hope by simply sharing their story, sharing their testimony of how God is the restorer and the realistic battle of porn addiction. Gerald and Tabby are passionate about normalizing conversations of the distortion of sex and intimacy among married couples and those that will be married. The hope is that all that are ‘captives come out, and to those in darkness, be free.’ (Isaiah 49:9)
